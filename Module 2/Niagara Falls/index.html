Visit Niagara Falls

Enjoy the sights, take a wine tour, or play some golf. Just a few of the great things to do in the "Falls"!!

Three great activities to do while in Niagara

"Sail" on the Maid of the Mist

Get right up close to the thundering sound of the mighty cataract

Visit the Skylon Tower

View the falls from on high, and have a meal in the Revolving Dining Room

Take a walk on Clifton Hill

Enjoy all of the asmusement park-like games and rides!
